Implemented the optimized kernel in [output_model_new.py](/workspace/kb_eval_pipeline/runs/kb1/p09/optimization_rounds/round2/output_model_new.py).

The kernel keeps the required 4-wave MFMA mapping and rewrites the fixed `K=32` path as a software-pipelined two-stage schedule: preload the first `K=16` slice into LDS, fetch the second slice from global while the first MFMA step is running, write that second slice into the alternate LDS buffer between the two MFMA instructions, then consume the second buffer after a barrier. That gives a double-buffered, unrolled-by-2 execution without changing the MFMA swizzle or accumulator writeback mapping.
